Project Details

The objective of this project is to develop a comprehensive website catering to both students and teachers, featuring distinct dashboards for each user group. The teacher dashboard will enable instructors to post assignments, share instructional materials, and facilitate interactive learning experiences. On the other hand, the student dashboard will provide easy access to these resources, allowing students to watch instructional videos and access supplementary learning materials. By creating separate yet interconnected dashboards, this website aims to foster a collaborative and efficient learning environment, where teachers can effectively communicate and engage with their students, while students can easily access and benefit from the provided instructional content.
